6. The vendor shall execute the conveyance deed in favour of the purchaser or his nominee as the purchaser may require, on receipt of the balance consideration.

8. The expenses for, preparation of the conveyance deed, cost of stamp, registration charges and all other cut of pocket expenses shall be borne by the purchaser.
